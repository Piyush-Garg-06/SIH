Digital Health Records for Migrant Workers

Ensuring accessible healthcare for all migrant workers in Kerala through a centralized digital health record system.

Key Features of the System

Unique Health ID

Each migrant worker receives a unique health ID and digital health card for lifetime medical records.

Centralized Records

All medical history stored securely in one place, accessible to authorized healthcare providers.

Health Alerts

Receive SMS/email notifications for checkups, vaccination reminders, and health advisories.

Disease Prevention

Early detection and tracking of communicable diseases to protect public health.

Scheme Benefits

Direct access to government health schemes without intermediaries or paperwork.

Multilingual Support

Available in multiple languages including Hindi, Bengali, Odia, and Malayalam.
